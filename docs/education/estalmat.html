ESTALMAT

ESTALMAT is a Spanish program for the promotion and development of math and reasoning skills among children and teenagers. Promoted by the Spanish Royal Academy of Exact, Physical and Natural Sciences, the program offers extracurricular intensive math classes.

Although the program is very exclusive (only ~25 places per year and region), it gives everyone a fair chance and is completely free. The first and second year the sessions are weekly; while for the third and fourth years the sessions are once every two weeks.

I managed to qualify for the Valencian Community's ESTALMAT back in 2015, after participating in the Valencian Olympiad in Mathematics, which sparking my eventual passion for maths and computer science.

I remember my time at ESTALMAT very fondly. It was truly a remarkable experience, filled with amazing teachers and students alike.
