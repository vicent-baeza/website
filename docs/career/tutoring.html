Private Tutor
C++JavaPythonMASM Assembly

Gave Programming and Computer Engineering lessons to first, second and third year students from the University of Alicante. Lesson content was tailored to every student's needs, and varied greatly from student to student.

Taught topics include:

  • Basic Programming Concepts: If-statements, Loops, Functions, Classes, Inheritance, etc.
  • Math: Binary (2s complement), Calculus, Matrix Algebra, Discrete Math and Statistics
  • Basic Data Structures: Vectors, Linked Lists, Sets, Dictionaries, Stacks, Queues and Priority Queues
  • Graph Algorithms: BFS, DFS, A* search, Dijkstra's, Kruskal's, Beam Search and Iterative Deepening
  • Theory of computation: Finite-State Machines, Context-Free Grammars and Turing Machines
  • Optimization & Efficiency: Big-O Notation, Algorithm Analysis, Parallel Processing and Multithreading
  • Competitive Programming: Dynamic Programming, Greedy Algorithms, Divide & Conquer and Branch & Cut
  • Advanced Data Structures: Heaps, BSTs, AVL trees, Union Finds, Segment Trees, Tries and Graphs
  • Computer Architecture: Memory Management, Pipelining and MASM Assembly (32-bit)

Depending on the student, the lessons were given in C++, Java, Python, or a combination of the three.
